MY HOMETOWN - HANOI




GENERAL INFORMATION

Vietnam's capital, Hanoi, is among the world's oldest capitals and features both classical and modern architecture right in the heart of the city.

a well-known location for local cuisine, a place with interesting landmarks and friendly residents, and a varied area with a mix of different communities.




HANOI WEATHER

The best months to visit Hanoi are April through June, but May is an especially lovely month since that is when the trees suddenly burst into blossom. Visits are also highly recommended in September and October when the weather is cooler and the sky is clearer.
